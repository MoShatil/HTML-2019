About Me

I'm Mohammad Shatil, but I go by Shatil, I'm currently an NYU undergrad in Integrated Digital Media.

I'm Bengali, of South Asian descent, and if theres anything I can tell you about Bangladesh, it's that the foods amazing, but traffic's horrible!

I was raised in NYC and got to experience my life surrounded by tons of culture.

I love to express my creative side and try many different things in order to broaden my horizons.

Aside from dabbling in the arts I love to keep up with basketball, cook something new whenever I can, or just explore!

My main interest is in Film and Game Design, being exposed to media really has peaked my personal interests.
